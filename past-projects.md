# Reference 10. Automotive Audio Amplifier Tier-1

**Customer:** Bose

## Challenges / Objective
- Validation of initial audio amplifier (DP1) prototype
- Design updates of subsequent rounds (DV & PV)
- Validation of DV round

## Solution / Scope
- Align DP1 and DV Test plan as per Client OEM
- Perform validation on DP1 boards
- Update schematic and BOM
- Perform simulations as needed
- Incorporate test findings and update layout files
- Update mechanical design with DP1 findings
- Build prototype assemblies in appropriate quantities for DV (~200)
- Perform DV testing
- Review DV test report and update PV design for final mock & validation by Client

## Client Benefits / Value Delivered
- Overall project execution within client budget in timely manner
- Automation to speed up execution and provide greater consistency

## Platform
- Automotive Audio Amplifier

## Key Features / Complexities
- Electrical Bench Validation: Power, Signal Integrity, Audio and Hi-sweep measurements
- Mechanical Bench Validation: Thermal Cycling, Stress, Strain and Structural analysis
- Manufacturing: PCBA, mechanical enclosures, test fixture (Shock, Vibration, Load and Strain)
- Compliance: EMI/EMC, Mechanical, Environmental, Life Test

## Tools
- Mechanical: Solidworks 2010, AutoCAD
- HW: OrCAD for Schematics and Allegro for Layout
- CAM 350 for Gerber verification, Hyperlynx for SI analysis and PI, ADS tool for full wave simulation
- V&V: LabView, Python, .NET
